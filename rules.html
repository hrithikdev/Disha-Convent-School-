Rules and Regulations

  1. All students should come in a neat and clean uniform with their identity cards.
  2. Students must reach the school at least ten minutes before the morning assembly.
  3. In case of absence, applications for leave should be submitted. Only those applications that are duly signed by a parent or guardian will be considered genuine.
  4. Students must speak in English on the school campus.
  5. Parents/guardians are not allowed to see or meet their wards or teachers during study hours without the principal's permission each time.
  6. Students must take part in all extracurricular activities assigned to them by the school. Parents' cooperation is required in this regard.
  7. No student is allowed to leave the school premises during school hours without proper permission from the principal. They are forbidden from purchasing anything from outside. Children will only be allowed to leave the school with a specific written request regarding their ward's progress.
  8. Parents are requested to cooperate in academic matters by ensuring regularity, discipline, and taking a general interest in their ward's progress.
  9. If a child misbehaves with any member of the school, the school has every right to take action against him/her.
  10. Discipline and decent behavior are given the highest importance. Every student is expected to obey the teachers and follow the rules and regulations.
  11. The school diary must be brought to school every class day.
  12. Children are not allowed to keep money with them. No pupil is allowed to wear costly jewelry, carry electronic gadgets, or bring any expensive articles to school.
  13. Students should be regular in attendance.
  14. The use of profane or insulting language and immoral behavior will be considered a serious offense.
  15. In case of a long leave due to medical reasons, a medical certificate must be attached to the application.
  16. Parents are usually allowed to meet teachers only on PTM days. They may meet the principal from 9:00 AM to 10:00 AM with a prior appointment.
  17. The school does not hold any responsibility, legal or otherwise, for unforeseen accidents in the school or while traveling on buses and tours. However, the school will take all necessary steps to prevent such incidents.
