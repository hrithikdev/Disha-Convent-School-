Welcome to the Website of Disha Convent School, Varanasi

Established in 2013, Disha Convent School has emerged as a leading CBSE school in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, committed to academic excellence and holistic development. As one of the top convent schools in Varanasi, we focus on quality education, character building, and strong moral values, fostering a dynamic and engaging learning environment. Our student-centric approach, combined with modern teaching methodologies, prepares young minds for future success and competitive challenges.

Affiliated with the CBSE board, Disha Convent School offers a structured and innovative curriculum from pre-primary to twelfth grade, emphasizing intellectual growth, critical thinking, and ethical values. Recognized as one of the best CBSE schools in Varanasi, we provide personalized learning experiences, ensuring that every student receives individual attention and academic guidance to excel in school education and personal development.

Disha Convent School operates across two well-equipped campuses in Varanasi, providing students with a stimulating and enriching learning environment:

  • Mahmoorganj Branch: Catering to students up to 8th grade, this campus focuses on strong foundational learning, fostering curiosity, creativity, and skill development in a student-friendly atmosphere.
  • Marhauli Branch: Located in the serene surroundings of Varanasi Enclave, this campus extends up to 12th grade, offering advanced academic training and comprehensive college preparation to help students excel in higher education.

Both campuses feature state-of-the-art infrastructure, spacious classrooms, and modern technology, ensuring a well-rounded educational experience that nurtures academic excellence, innovation, and holistic growth.

At Disha Convent School, education goes beyond textbooks. We encourage students to engage in extracurricular activities, including cultural programs, sports, and community initiatives, fostering well-rounded personalities. Our value-based education system instills essential life skills like time management, teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving, ensuring students are equipped for real-world challenges.

We believe in active parental involvement and maintain open communication to foster a supportive school community. Our engaging digital presence on platforms like Facebook keeps parents informed about school events, achievements, and daily activities.

With an unwavering commitment to academic success and personal growth, Disha Convent School continues to empower students, shaping them into confident, responsible, and future-ready leaders in a rapidly evolving world.
